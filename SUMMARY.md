# Project Summary

## Mission

Build a body that lets you surf better every year.

## Two Time Horizons

The manual helps a surfer prepare for the next meaningful performance
opportunity while developing the movement quality, recovery capacity, relative
strength, aerobic fitness, paddle capacity, durability, and habits needed for
lifelong improvement. The [Project North Star](docs/foundations/project-north-star.md)
is the canonical statement of this philosophy.

## Major Systems

1. Assessment
2. Recovery
3. Movement and mobility
4. Strength
5. Conditioning and paddling
6. Surf athleticism
7. Nutrition
8. Programming
9. Exercise library

Programs apply these systems to a block, season, or trip. They link to canonical
protocols rather than repeating them.

## Repository Rules

- Markdown is canonical; sites and PDFs are generated artifacts.
- The public repository contains reusable knowledge, not personal athlete data.
- Concepts and protocols are defined once and linked elsewhere.
- Expert attribution follows the approved source list.
- Material outside that list is labeled general exercise physiology.
- Significant decisions are recorded in `docs/decisions/`.
