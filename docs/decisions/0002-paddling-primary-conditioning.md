# Decision 0002: Paddling Is the Primary Sport-Specific Conditioning

## Status

Accepted

## Context

Surfing requires repeated paddling efforts, position tolerance, and the ability
to recover between efforts and sessions. General conditioning can support these
qualities but does not reproduce the full task.

## Decision

Paddling is the primary sport-specific conditioning method. Other conditioning
methods support paddle and surf capacity without displacing recoverable water or
paddle volume.

## Consequences

- Strength and conditioning programming accommodates paddle volume.
- Easy paddling may accumulate volume with limited fatigue.
- Long paddles and paddle intervals are progressed deliberately.
- Paddle volume is adjusted when readiness or peak timing requires it.
