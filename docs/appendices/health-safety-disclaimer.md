## Educational Purpose

The Surf Athlete Manual provides general educational information about physical
preparation, recovery, movement, strength, conditioning, nutrition, and surfing
performance. It is not medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, rehabilitation,
individualized nutrition care, or a substitute for qualified professional care.

The manual does not create a clinician-patient, coach-athlete, or other
professional relationship.

## Individual Decisions

Exercise and surfing involve risk. A method that is appropriate for one person
may be inappropriate for another because of health history, injury, medication,
training experience, environment, or other individual factors.

Consult an appropriately qualified healthcare professional before starting or
changing training when health status, symptoms, injury, or medical conditions
create uncertainty. Use qualified coaching or technical instruction when a
movement, exercise, or water environment exceeds current skill or experience.

## Stop and Seek Appropriate Care

Do not use the manual, a readiness score, or a recovery technique to ignore or
conceal pain, illness, injury concerns, or new, severe, worsening, or unusual
symptoms. Stop or modify the activity and seek appropriate evaluation. Use local
emergency services for a medical emergency.

## Research and Uncertainty

The manual distinguishes accepted systems from research under evaluation.
Research citations, expert attribution, and general exercise physiology do not
guarantee that a recommendation is complete, current, or appropriate for a
particular person. Evidence and professional guidance can change. Uncertainty
should be stated rather than hidden.

## AI-Assisted Use

An AI assistant can help organize information and apply the manual's systems,
but its output may be incomplete or incorrect. AI output is not a medical
evaluation or a replacement for qualified professional judgment. Keep personal
health and training information in a private workspace and review the privacy
terms of any service used.
