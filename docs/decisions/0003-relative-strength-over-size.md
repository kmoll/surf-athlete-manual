# Decision 0003: Relative Strength Over Muscle Size

## Status

Accepted

## Context

Surfing rewards useful force production, movement quality, durability, and an
efficient strength-to-mass relationship. Training organized primarily around
hypertrophy can add fatigue or mass without proportional surf benefit.

## Decision

Strength programming prioritizes relative strength, movement quality,
durability, and low-fatigue force production rather than muscle size as an end
in itself.

## Consequences

- Heavy work remains low volume.
- Training rarely approaches failure.
- Exercise selection must support surfing or durability.
- Body composition is an outcome, not the central training goal.
