## Purpose

Treat recovery as a governing constraint on training so that useful stress
produces adaptation rather than unnecessary fatigue.

## Governing Rule

Recovery has priority over planned volume. The schedule is adjusted when current
capacity cannot support the intended work with appropriate quality.

## Guidance

- [Recovery-Readiness Guidance](readiness-guidance.md): choose whether to
  proceed, modify, recover, or evaluate without using a readiness score.

## Scope

This section owns reusable recovery principles and decision guidance. Personal
sleep, symptoms, schedules, measurements, and recovery logs belong in the
private application workspace.
