## Purpose

Develop usable range of motion that supports paddling positions, pop-ups,
stance, force production, and durable movement in the water.

## Scope

This section owns reusable mobility principles, assessments, and routines.
Movement-pattern quality belongs in [Movement](../movement/index.md), while
programs specify when a mobility method is applied.

System content is in development.
