---
title: "Decision 0007: Programs Reference Systems"
---

## Status

Accepted

## Context

Programs schedule reusable exercises and protocols. Copying full instructions
into each program creates duplication and makes revisions inconsistent.

## Decision

Systems and exercise-library pages own reusable methods. Programs specify their
timing, selection, and adjustment and link to the canonical instructions.

## Consequences

- Protocol changes occur in one place.
- Program pages remain focused on scheduling and decisions.
- Links must be maintained when canonical pages move.
