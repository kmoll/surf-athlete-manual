---
title: "Decision 0001: Recovery Comes First"
---

## Status

Accepted

## Context

Surf training may combine water time, paddle volume, strength work,
conditioning, and movement practice. Adding all planned work without regard for
adaptation can reduce performance and long-term consistency.

## Decision

Recovery is the highest programming priority. Training volume and intensity are
reduced when sleep, soreness, illness, joint symptoms, or accumulated fatigue
indicate inadequate recovery.

## Consequences

- Planned work is adjusted rather than completed blindly.
- Deloads are part of the system.
- Strength work avoids unnecessary fatigue.
- Most weeks preserve capacity for one additional hard session.
