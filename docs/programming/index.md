## Purpose

Show how reusable systems are combined across sessions, weeks, training blocks,
performance opportunities, and long-term cycles.

## Scope

This section owns phase structure, weekly templates, split-session rules,
deloads, peaks, travel adjustments, post-event recovery, and annual planning.
Programs use relative timing and generic athlete roles.

Programs link to canonical system and exercise pages instead of reproducing
their instructions.
